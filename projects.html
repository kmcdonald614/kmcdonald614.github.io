In my deep learning class I had an assignment where I was given a data set on the different species of Iris and I had to make 5 different networks to have the computer predict the Iris at a certain row in the dataset. First I had to Import the data into my .py file, then I had to convert all the categorical data in the dataset to numerical data. Once I manipulate the data to how I plan on having the computer learn, I start creating my networks. I had to create 5 different networks with different dense layers and dropout rates and layer activation functions to determine which network is the most accurate and can predict the Iris species of a certain row in the dataset. These networks are shown below.

Below is my results where I predict a species at a certain point in the dataset. I have a graph that shows the validation loss and the training loss.
